Part 1 · Recap

What AI Is — and Isn't

GOOD AT

  • First drafts & rewrites
  • Summaries & structure
  • Options and ideas
  • Pattern spotting
  • Reformatting content

NOT GOOD AT

  • Guaranteed truth
  • Private judgement
  • Legal certainty
  • Emotional nuance
  • Accountability

Part 1 · Recap

The Prompt Formula

Role + Context + Task + Constraints + Output
  • Act as the useful expert (role)
  • Explain the context and background
  • Please ask for the specific task + format
  • Weak prompts → add role, context, goal, tone, format
  • Use AI as a thinking partner: options, trade-offs, risks, blind spots

Part 2 · Recap

Four Models — When to Use Each

  • ChatGPT — versatile all-rounder for most business work
  • Gemini — best when your work lives in Google Workspace
  • Claude — long, careful writing and sensitive documents
  • Grok — real-time, internet- and X-aware tasks

Part 2 · Recap

Beyond the Chatbots

  • NotebookLM — grounded research; answers only from your sources, with citations
  • Gamma — generate polished decks and one-pagers in minutes
  • Google AI Studio — the power-user Gemini playground with system instructions

Part 3 · Recap

Safe AI Use

  • Before you paste: never share confidential, personal or financial data without approved systems
  • Turn off training in each tool's data settings
  • Safe Context Rewriter: strip names, figures and addresses — keep the problem intact
  • AI helps day-to-day: summarise, draft, rewrite, organise, ideate and check

Part 4 · Recap

Agents, Ethics & Action

  • Chat → Workflow → Agent: understand the progression
  • Agents can research, triage inboxes, schedule and automate — with human oversight
  • Trust, but check: names, dates, numbers, sources, claims
  • Would this be fair to everyone? check for exclusion and bias
  • Leave with a 7-day action plan — improve one thing, not everything

Don’t transform everything.
Improve one thing.
