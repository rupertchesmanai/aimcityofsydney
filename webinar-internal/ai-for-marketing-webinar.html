Part 1 · Recap

The New Marketing Stack

  • ChatGPT — strategy & copy
  • ChatGPT Images / Midjourney / Nano Banana — visuals
  • Canva — branded design
  • AI helps marketers: draft, repurpose, plan, visualise, check
  • Safe AI for marketing: keep customer data out unless approved

Part 1 · Recap

The Marketing Prompt Formula

Role + Audience + Goal + Tone + Format
  • Bad prompt → better prompt: add who it's for and what success looks like
  • Specify channel, length and call-to-action
  • Your persona + brand voice are your secret weapon in every prompt

Part 2 · Recap

Audience & Voice

  • Customer persona — a reusable avatar to paste into prompts
  • Brand voice block — keeps AI sounding like you
  • Avoid AI slop — edit for specificity, proof and personality
  • Use AI for customer messages and review replies — then check tone

Part 2 · Recap

One Idea, Five Posts

  • Repurpose one idea across channels (post, email, caption, carousel, short)
  • ChatGPT for campaign angles and strategy
  • Keep a swipe file of prompts that work for your brand

Part 3 · Recap

Visual Marketing

  • Choose the right tool: ChatGPT Images, Midjourney, Nano Banana, Canva
  • Image prompt formula: subject + style + composition + lighting + format
  • Build a repeatable visual workflow from concept → generate → refine → brand in Canva

Part 4 · Recap

Campaigns, Trust & Wrap-up

  • 30-day campaign builder — a month of content from one brief
  • Ready to publish? run the pre-publish checklist
  • Trust, but check facts, claims and numbers
  • Would this be fair? check for stereotypes and exclusion
  • Leave with your final marketing kit — persona, voice, content, visuals, campaign
